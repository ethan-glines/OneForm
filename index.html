Built for Security, Records, and Integration

🔒

Enterprise-Grade Security

OneForm inherits your tenant's existing security policies — Azure AD, conditional access, MFA, and DLP. No third-party exposure. No separate admin console. Your IT team already controls everything.

📂

Managed Records Lifecycle

Every submission automatically becomes a managed record with retention policies, audit trails, and disposition tracking. Records are governed from creation to archival — not piling up in folders.

🔗

Native Integrations

Power Automate for workflows. Power BI for reporting. Teams for notifications. SharePoint for storage. OneForm connects to what you already use — no APIs to configure, no middleware to maintain.
